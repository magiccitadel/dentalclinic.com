What is a Teeth Cleaning?

Teeth cleaning, or dental prophylaxis, is a professional service provided by our experienced dental team. During the cleaning, we remove plaque and tartar that have built up on your teeth. Even with regular brushing and flossing, some areas in your mouth are hard to reach, making professional cleanings essential for optimal oral health. make an appointment today

Why Choose [Your Clinic Name] for Your Teeth Cleaning?

Prevention of Cavities: Plaque is the leading cause of tooth decay. At [Your Clinic Name], we effectively remove plaque and tartar to protect your teeth from cavities and keep your smile healthy.

Gum Disease Prevention: Our comprehensive cleanings help prevent gum disease by removing irritants like plaque and tartar that can cause inflammation and infection. We focus on early detection and treatment to ensure your gums stay healthy.

Fresh Breath: Persistent bad breath can be embarrassing. Our teeth cleaning service helps eliminate the bacteria responsible for bad breath, leaving your mouth feeling fresh and clean.

Brighten Your Smile: We take pride in helping you achieve a bright, confident smile. Our professional cleanings remove surface stains, enhancing the appearance of your teeth and boosting your self-esteem.
